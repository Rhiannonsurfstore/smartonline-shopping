# Challenges Faced

During the design and development of the Smart Online Shopping prototype, our group encountered several challenges. These challenges helped us improve our design, collaboration, and problem-solving skills.

---

## 1. Learning Advanced Figma Features

Initially, it was challenging to use features such as Auto Layout, Components, and Prototyping. We practiced using these tools and gradually improved our skills.

---

## 2. Maintaining Design Consistency

Keeping the same colors, fonts, button styles, and spacing across all screens required careful attention. We created reusable design elements to maintain consistency.

---

## 3. Designing User-Friendly Navigation

We wanted users to move easily from one screen to another. We improved the navigation by organizing menus, buttons, and page transitions clearly.

---

## 4. Creating the Interactive Prototype

Connecting every screen correctly required careful testing. We reviewed each interaction and fixed navigation issues until the prototype worked smoothly.

---

## 5. Organizing the GitHub Repository

Structuring the repository with folders, Markdown documentation, screenshots, and links required careful organization to meet the assignment requirements.

---

## Lessons Learned

Through this project, we improved our skills in:

- UI/UX Design
- Figma
- Wireframing
- Interactive Prototyping
- GitHub Repository Management
- Team Collaboration

---

## Conclusion

Despite the challenges, we successfully completed the Smart Online Shopping prototype and gained valuable practical experience in designing modern user-centered applications.
